About Lake Kivu

Lake Kivu is one of the largest and most beautiful lakes in Rwanda, known for its calm waters and scenic views.

Activities

  • Boat rides
  • Swimming
  • Kayaking
  • Fishing
  • Beach relaxation

Places to Stay

Lake Kivu offers hotels, lodges, and guest houses with beautiful lake views.

Popular accommodations include Lake Kivu Serena Hotel, Cormoran Lodge, and Kivu Peace View Hotel.
