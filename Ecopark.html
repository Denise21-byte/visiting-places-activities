Ecopark is a large urban park located in the outskirts of the city, offering a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of urban life. The park features lush greenery, walking trails, picnic areas, and a variety of recreational activities for visitors of all ages. It is an ideal destination for nature lovers, families, and anyone looking to unwind in a peaceful environment.

Different activities at Ecopark

  • Nature walks and hiking
  • Bird watching
  • Picnicking
  • Cycling
  • Outdoor yoga and meditation
